LOCATIONS

Hamtramck Manufactory

Our funky little flagship store in our hometown of Hamtramck, right in the heart of Detroit! Here, in the most diverse city of the state with the second most diverse agricultural economy in the country, we think we are optimally situated to create, explore flavors and hang with some of the coolest neighbors a chocolate shop could ever ask for! (Oloman! Fat Salmon Sushi!)

We are at the corner of Joseph Campau and Caniff, across from the Islamic Cultural Center and The Euromart.

Downtown Detroit

We bring fresh chocolate every day to our Downtown Detroit showroom,  just a block away from the center of the city! Find us at the corner of Fort & Griswold (on the Fort Street side!) in the historic old Dime Building, now known as “The Chrysler House”. Our tiny little shop is thrilled to make a chocolate-y little smudge on the history of the downtown shopping district.

To find us, look across Fort Street from The Penobscot Building, next to The Federal Reserve!

Pro Tip: We are in the same building as The Dime Store and you don’t want to miss their brunch!

Saturday-Sunday 10-3
